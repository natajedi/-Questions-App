CSS Interview Questions You Should Prepare

2. What do you understand by the universal sector?

7. What is the difference between a class and an ID?

8. How can you target h3 and h2 with the same styling?

9. How can you use CSS to control image repetition?

10. Tell us about the property used for image scroll controlling?

11. What are the limitations of CSS?

12. How to include CSS in the webpage?

13. What are the different types of Selectors in CSS?

14. What is a CSS Preprocessor? What are Sass, Less, and Stylus? Why do people use them?

15. What is VH/VW (viewport height/ viewport width) in CSS?

16. Difference between reset vs normalize CSS? How do they differ?

17. Is it important to test the webpage in different browsers?

18. What are Pseudo classes?

19. How do you specify units in the CSS? What are the different ways to do it?

20. Does margin-top or margin-bottom have an effect on inline elements?
